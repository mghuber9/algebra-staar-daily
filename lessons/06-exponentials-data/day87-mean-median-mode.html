Day 87 – Mean/Median/Mode

Goal: Practice skills with Mean/Median/Mode.

← Back to all lessons
Key Idea (Mean/Median/Mode):
• Mean = average (sum ÷ number of values)
• Median = middle value
• Mode = most frequent value

Example (General Algebra)

Find the mean, median, and mode of: 4, 7, 7, 10, 13

  • Mean: (4 + 7 + 7 + 10 + 13) ÷ 5 = 41 ÷ 5 = 8.2
  • Median: middle value = 7
  • Mode: 7 appears most → mode = 7

Your Turn (General Algebra)

Find the median of: 3, 8, 1, 9, 5

Show answer

Put in order: 1, 3, 5, 8, 9 → median = 5.

STAAR-Style Practice

1. What is the mean of: 12, 15, 9, 6?

  1. 9
  2. 10.5
  3. 12
  4. 42

2. What is the mode of: 3, 5, 5, 7, 7, 7, 8?

  1. 3
  2. 5
  3. 7
  4. 8

3. What is the median of: 14, 7, 10, 3, 8?

  1. 8
  2. 7
  3. 10
  4. 14
Show STAAR-style answers and explanations

1. Mean = (12+15+9+6)/4 = 42/4 = 10.5 → B

2. Most frequent is 7 → C

3. Ordered: 3,7,8,10,14 → median = 8 → A

Always sort the data before finding median or mode.
