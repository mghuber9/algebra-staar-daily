Daily STAAR Algebra Lessons

Short 5-minute Algebra I practice lessons aligned to STAAR.

How this site works

Each school day, students complete one quick micro-lesson: a short explanation, an example, and a few practice problems.

Teachers can link directly to any lesson from Canvas, Google Classroom, or other LMS platforms.
